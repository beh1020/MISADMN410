Financing Policies

We accept all forms of payment in both of our store locations: cash, credit, and debit cards. For large orders, we will accept checks made out to Durham House of Pets with the invoice number on the memo line.

For any purchases greater than $1250.00, we can work out a payment plan. Most likely it will come out to be 1% APR with 24 equal payments over two years. The minimum monthly payment is 5% of the qualifying purchase. Payments can be made in our regional offices or in-store in either the Durham or Portsmouth location.

Delivery Policies

We will utilize UPS and FedEx shipping and delivery services. You should expect to receive your deliveries between 5-7 business days of your order date.
Please note that we cannot ship to a PO Box. Alternatively, you may have your order shipped to either store location to pick it up.

Premium Delivery Service--For an extra shipping fee, we can ensure that your delivery gets to you within three days of your order.

We acknowledge that products may be damaged in transit or be defective. If you receive any products with damages or defects, the Durham House of Pets team will work with you on replacing your item(s), free of charge. To utilize this benefit, the damage must be reported within two days of delivery.

Return Policies

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you must return the item(s) within 30 days of the original purchase at either the Portsmouth or Durham location with your receipt. Refunds will be issued in the same form as the original payment method. If returned between 30-60 days of the original purchase with your receipt, and the products are either (a) unopened or (b) in lightly-used condition, you can receive a refund in the form of store credit.

Durham House of Pets is committed to providing our customers with reliable and satisfactory service with every purchase.
